JC Techie is the **digital plant chemistry** side of Juschem Solutions Pvt. Ltd. — the same expertise that drives our audits and training, made continuous and quantitative.

## Why this exists

Every plant we audit has the same problem: the data needed to run good chemistry is already there. It's spread across DCS tags, lab notebooks, online analyser streams, and standalone dashboards that don't talk to each other. By the time an issue surfaces in a quarterly review, the corrosion or scaling event has already cost money.

JC Techie closes that gap. It is a chemistry-aware monitoring layer — not a generic analytics platform — purpose-built for steam-water cycles, RO trains, and cooling systems.

## What's in the platform

- A single chemistry view spanning **boiler steam-water cycle**, **RO and pre-treatment**, and **circulating cooling water**.
- Rule sets aligned to **IAPWS**, **EPRI**, and **VGB** guidelines — the same standards used in our consulting engagements.
- Drift detection and excursion alerts routed to whoever owns chemistry on shift.
- A monthly chemistry report assembled automatically from operational data.
